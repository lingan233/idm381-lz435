2. Tobii Eye Tracker 2D Game

  • Link: Official Website, Amazon
  • Price: $239
  • Availability: Amazon Prime 2 day delivery
  • What does this technology do?
    • Eye and head tracking
  • What do you want to do with technology?
    • It is a 2D game where the player will move their eye in a certain direction to control the character in the game to avoid obstacles and gain rewards. The game will constantly move horizontally to the right and the player needs to move their eye vertically to win the game. The game will have a score after the player loses.

3. Leap Motion Rock Paper Scissor

  • Link: Official Website
  • Price: $139
  • Availability: UltraLeap Official Website delivery
  • What does this technology do?
    • Hand motion tracking
  • What do you want to do with technology?
    • 100% fair rock paper scissor simulator with this hand motion tracking device. In real real-life rock paper scissor game, 2 players can have a slight difference in the speed which they show their hand gestures, but in this simulator, the 2 players can play online while the computer stores the result and shows the result after both players decided which hand gesture to use for this round.
