Leap-War: Hand vs. Hand

The Overview

  • This project aimed to leverage the new technology leap motion to create a trend to adapt leap motion in gaming. The project focused on enhancing user experience. This case study details the challenges faced, the design process adopted, the final solution and the measurable results achieved.

Context and Challenge

  • Description: The existing games could improve their use of the leap motion.
  • Timeline: The project takes 10 weeks as a class project.
  • Purpose: Enhance user experience, and incorporate the leap motion.
  • The Problem: The primary challenge was to incorporate the leap motion and create a meaning product.
  • Goal & Objectives: Embrace growth and innovation in gesture-based gaming; Design the figma pages for simple and intuitive gaming with good gameplay

Process and Insight

  • Competitive Analysis: Compared multiple games and websites that used leap motion.
  • Wireframes and Prototypes: Created wireframes and interactive prototypes to visualize and iterate on the design.
  • Style Guide: Established a comprehensive style guide to maintain design consistency across the platform.

The Solution

  • A revolutionary leap motion game transforming the traditional rock paper scissor game, allowing for both friends and strangers to play online.

The Results

  • Success Metrics:
    • Interesting gameplay
    • Good graphic and interface
    • Meaningful integration of the hand motion.
  • Lessons Learned:
    • Learned more about different new technologies.
    • Acknowledged the need of research in design.
