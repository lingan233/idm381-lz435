Leap Motion Rock Paper Scissors

1. RPS Island

  • In RPS Island, there are different enemies which you need to make different hand gestures to fight with them. Aim the enemies with your eye, and make ur hand guesture to win them.
  • Link: RPS Island
  • What do they do well?
    • It is good at integrating VR and Leap Motion together.
    • It has interesting graphics.
  • What do they NOT do well?
    • Seems like a boring game.
    • Cannot play with friends.
    • Too simple of a gameplay.
  • Is the software making the best use (UXID) of the hardware or is it just doing the "Same O Same O"?
    • No, the software is not making the best use of the hardware because this game is overall not as interesting using the rock paper scissor game mechanic. The rock paper scissor is not a meaningful interaction; instead, it is used as a pure hand spell. It is the “Same O Same O” for a regular adventure game but managed to add the leap motion and the rock paper scissors.

2. Virtual Strangers

  • Virtual Strangers puts two complete strangers, from anywhere in the world, into a shared virtual space and gives them the ability to talk to each other, create drawings together, and scale them up to mind-bending proportions, all using the most natural and intuitive instruments humans have, their own hands.
  • Link: Virtual Strangers
  • What do they do well?
    • The gameplay is interesting and creative.
    • The interaction between players is fun.
    • The integration with the hand motion seems to create lots of freedom for players to create whatever shapes they want.
  • What do they NOT do well?
    • The art style doesn't feel very finished.
    • The mouth part doesn't seem to create much meaning.
  • Is the software making the best use (UXID) of the hardware or is it just doing the "Same O Same O"?
    • This game makes the best use of the hardware because it uses the leap motion in a meaningful and creative way. The game cannot really exist without the technology. The core game mechanic is strongly related to the leap motion.

  • What do they NOT do well?
    • N/A

What I want to do with my project

  • A multiplayer rock paper scissor game where players can connect with their friends online and use leap motion to record their hand gestures to play the game.
  • This game will have an aesthetically pleasing user-friendly interface.
