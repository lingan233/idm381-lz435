2. Virtual Strangers

  • Virtual Strangers puts two complete strangers, from anywhere in the world, into a shared virtual space and gives them the ability to talk to each other, create drawings together, and scale them up to mind-bending proportions, all using the most natural and intuitive instruments humans have, their own hands.
  • Link: Virtual Strangers
  • What do they do well?
    • The gameplay is interesting and creative.
    • The interaction between players is fun.
    • The integration with the hand motion seems to create lots of freedom for players to create whatever shapes they want.
  • What do they NOT do well?
    • The art style doesn't feel very finished.
    • The mouth part doesn't seem to create much meaning.
